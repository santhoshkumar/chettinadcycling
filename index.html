About the town

Karaikudi, the heart of Chettinad

Karaikudi is the largest town in the Chettinad region of Tamil Nadu, known for its grand mansions, Temples, hand-painted Athangudi tiles and quiet, flat backroads — ideal riding terrain for early morning distance.

The surrounding villages are linked by low-traffic roads that pass temples, tile workshops and century-old courtyards, which is what shapes almost every route we ride.

Why ride here

  • Flat, low-traffic roads through mansion villages
  • Relax riding window before 8 AM, most of the year
  • Heritage stops — palaces, temple towns
  • Easy loop-backs to Karaikudi for support and water
